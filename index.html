Aortic Valve PPM Calculator

Indexed EOA = EOA / BSA (Mosteller). Severe PPM ≤ 0.65; Moderate PPM 0.65–0.85 (cm²/m²).
BSA: — m²
Methodology acknowledgement.
Indexed effective orifice area (iEOA) is computed as EOA divided by body surface area (Mosteller formula).
Prosthesis-specific EOA values were curated from published hemodynamic datasets (preferably one-year follow-up) and mapped to labeled sizes and outside diameters.
PPM categories are derived from standard iEOA cutoffs (≤0.65 severe; 0.65–0.85 moderate cm²/m²).
